1.Delivery procedure rules and policies
Avenger provides delivery services to our customers within the 8km radius from the nearest restaurant. The distance can be changed depending on the customer’s location. Customers can easily verify whether their address is within the delivery service zone by checking our website delivery.avengers.com.
2.Delivery fee policies
Avenger currently providing FREE shipping for the orders which the distance are equal or less than 2km, or 30VND after VAT for ones which the distance are over 2km.
3.Delivery Time policies
a. Delivery time frame: Avenger commits to the latest delivery time as no later than the promised time once the customers submitted the orders. Customers will be notified and agreed upon any changes in timing whether it’s sooner or later than the promised time.
b. Delivery time: Time can be changed according to the setting of each restaurant, but the latest for customers to receive orders will always be 30 minutes before closing.
c. Upon delivery, when customers receive the order, please make the payment to the shipper, then check all of the items to ensure you are happy with the shipment. Please keep the receipt for future needs if any. In the case of non-cash payment, customers do not make the payment to shippers.
Note: Orders will be cancelled automatically if our shippers can not reach the customers at the time of delivery (maximum of 3 calls with 5 minutes increment between each call).
