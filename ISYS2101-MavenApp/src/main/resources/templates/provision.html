FARM TO RESTAURANT

"Farm to Restaurant"
Came about naturally from our desire to see happy diners

"What can we do to make individuals who dine with us happier?"
After giving that subject a lot of attention, we were compelled to pledge our dedication to the excellence of our components.
The very least, simply the beginning, is delicious meals. We will be able to witness more smiles if our food is delicious, safe, and healthy.
We want to disseminate "genuine happiness," and their smiles will be the entrance.

This is how we arrived at our "Farm to Restaurant" pledge.
It is a philosophy that encourages supplying safe and healthy food straight from the source (farm) to the customer (in Restaurant), all while maintaining consistent management.
Farmers in Da Lat who supply fresh vegetables directly to our restaurants because of the trust we have been able to establish with them. Our packaged items, such ham and canned tomatoes, are imported straight from Italy and come from carefully chosen companies.

A fundamental principle of ours is "Farm to Restaurant."
